Senior Risk Officer

Job Description

At Bibby Financial Services (BFS), marketing is about understanding the requirements of small and mid-size businesses — and marketing our products and services that address their financial needs. We’re looking for an experienced and versatile marketing manager who is hungry to take on the challenge and build and execute the marketing strategy from the ground up. 

The marketing manager responsibilities are focused on demand generation and raising the brand profile in target market segments.  This includes tracking and analyzing the performance of multi-channel multi-touch campaigns against goals, managing the marketing budget and ensuring that all marketing material is in line with BFS’ brand identity. To be successful in this role, you should have hands-on experience with marketing automation, web analytics tools and be able to turn creative ideas into effective campaigns.

Our ideal candidate has experience developing and executing marketing campaigns and is comfortable with day-to-day marketing activities, as well as long-term strategy, thriving with tight deadlines and changing needs. 

 

Responsibilities

  • Establish positioning, identify target audiences and develop marketing plans with specific objectives across different channels  (direct and intermediary) and segments.
  • Drive demand generation through conceptualizing and executing on multi-channel campaigns across the prospect and customer lifecycle, ensuring the alignment of communications and messaging across all channels
  • Gather customer and market insights to inform outreach strategies, increase customer conversions, and generate more qualified leads
  • Identify effectiveness and impact of current marketing initiatives with tracking and analysis, and optimize accordingly, complete with formal proposals and recommendations on tactics; accountable for monitoring and reporting on ROI for all marketing activities
  • Develop and execute content strategy aligned to buying journey and business objectives
  • Develop marketing material in French and English, from website content and social media ads to hard copy brochures and case studies; localization and some translation is required
  • Build strategic relationships and partner with key industry players and associations
  • Develop and deliver a communications program that supports customer experience and loyalty
  • Prepare and monitor the marketing budget on a quarterly and annual basis and allocate funds to reach optimal reults
  • Source and manage relationships with external suppliers/ agencies in terms of deliverables, timelines and costs

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience necessary
  • 10+ years of client service/ risk experience with existing credit authorities
  • Prior management experience
  • Ability to work as part of a team in an office environment and to act independently as required
  • Computer literate with excellent keyboard skills and competency with MS
  • Ability to learn new skills quickly
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills required
  • Strong customer service skills required

Key Skills

  • Thorough understanding of BFS' target market
  • Able to evaluate and credit decision Factoring and ABL credit within that market
  • Able to provide a value added level of risk management service and support to assigned offices in such areas as account management, governance, structuring, financial and collateral analysis, reporting requirements and loan documentation
  • Competent problem asset resolution skills and attendant bankruptcy (or similar) issues
  • Able to manage select accounts on a daily basis, if necessary
  • Strong working knowledge of GAAP and financial statement analysis
  • Strong working knowledge of collateral and the field exam process
  • Working knowledge of Factoring and ABL systems
  • Able to construct information databases and manage an exception reporting process
  • Thorough command of legal documentation and attendant legal issues
  • Computer literate with good keyboard skills; competent user of MS Office suite with an above average command of Excel

Send your resume + cover letter to hr@bibbyusa.com.
