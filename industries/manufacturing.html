Funding Manufacturing

We understand the pressures in cash flow and time in manufacturing, from purchasing supplies to producing orders. Often, your cash is heavily tied up in stock, raw materials and supply chain. Our flexible financing products have been developed with these challenges in mind to deliver industry leading support.

We understand your needs

  • Finance your invoices, enabling you to purchase components and raw materials
  • High concentration ledger management
  • Cash flow to keep your supply chain moving

Our commitment to your business

  • No credit history required
  • We'll give you the working capital you need within 48 hours of inquiry
  • Our funding solutions adapt and grow with your business

Your Manufacturing finance benefits

  • High concentration - we fund single debtor ledgers
  • Easier to manage seasonality
  • Credit insurance if required
  • Confidentiality - no need to disclose our involvement
  • No credit history required for start-ups
