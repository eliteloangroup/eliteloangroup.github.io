  • Switching Invoice Finance Providers

You already have a factoring partner, or you might have used factoring services before.

Its a great idea to undertake an annual financial health check

We think it’s a great idea to undertake a financial health check every year and that means reviewing your current provider’s offer with ours. We’re highly competitive and discrete so you can be assured that your customer will only know we’re working on your behalf, if you tell them, or allow us to.

We offer a 24/7 client service portal to keep you informed

We promise to save you time chasing up on payments, getting access to your cash in less than 24 hours and we offer a 24/7 online client service portal to keep you up to date. We also offer protection when your customers have a financial inability to pay and our relationship specialists can help review your importing or exporting needs to ensure you’re getting best value.

The Benefits of Invoice Factoring with us

  • Offers a fast, flexible source of funding which grows in line with your business sales
  • Improving your cash flow means you can spend more time focused on growing your business rather than chasing for payment
  • You get access to cash in 24 hours

  • Up to 90 percent of your invoice is advanced to you - when we receive payment from your customer, you receive the remainder
  • We can take on the role of your credit department or be as discrete as you want - your customers don't even need to know you work with us
  • We offer Credit Insurance and Bad Debt Protection for your peace of mind
