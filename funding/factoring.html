  • Invoice Factoring

Generating cash from your invoices with full back office support

Factoring Services are ideal for many businesses. You could be an established business or a start-up. You may already have a factoring partner or are new to factoring and exploring options to improve cash flow in your business. Whatever your reason, we’re here to help you grow your business.

Factoring is a simple approach which lets you access payments due to you, faster. And unlike other lines of credit, factoring grows in line with your business’s sales.

Download the What is Factoring guide

We offer revolving lines of credit and term loans for inventory, machinery and real estate

Bibby Financial Services offer up to 90 per cent of invoice value to you usually in less than 24 hours of you issuing the invoice to your customer. We’re happy to work with clients who send out invoices with payment terms of up to 120 days. We can also offer revolving lines of credit and term loans for inventory, machinery and real estate.

Because our approach is relationship-based, we get to know your business and your customers.

We’ve been offering factoring services for more than 15 years

We’ve been offering factoring services for more than 15 years, working with over 10,400 clients releasing more than $14bn of invoice payments each year to our clients.

We hold memberships in the Commercial Finance Association, the International Factoring Association, and the Turnaround Management Association. 

Is factoring right for your business?

  • Your company trades predominantly with other commercial businesses
  • Invoicing is done in arrears and payment terms are within 120 days of invoice date
  • The business would benefit from additional support through an outsourced credit control
