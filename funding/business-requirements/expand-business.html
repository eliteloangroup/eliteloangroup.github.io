We help you to expand and grow with our funding

We can help you to fund your projects and expansion plans with Invoice Factoring and Asset Based Lending, and support your business ambitions.

We understand that a lack of cash flow can constrain a business of any size. If you find yourself waiting to be paid for the work you’ve done, goods you’ve sold or are spending more time than you would like chasing late payments from customers, then Invoice Finance could be for you.

Alternatively, Asset Based Lending allows you to invest in your business without tying up existing cash. It gives you the flexibility to purchase the equipment you need with affordable repayments helping to manage your cash flow. Or, you can unlock the value of your existing assets to release cash to further develop your business or improve your cash flow and stability. 

How Invoice Finance can help your business

Invoice Factoring is ideal for businesses that have customers on long payment terms, or who often pay late. Each can create a cash flow gap when it comes to paying staff and suppliers while waiting for invoices to be paid. It can also help businesses who want to take on new projects without taking on extra debt.

With our Factoring solution, you benefit from funding and credit control.

Invoice Finance

Features

  • Release up to 97% of your invoice value within 24 hours
  • Get cash against the value of your invoices as they are raised
  • We can take on the role of your credit department or be as discrete as you want - your customers don't need to know you work with us
  • We can Pre-Approve Clients' Credit
  • We also offer protection when your customers have a financial inability to pay
  • Manage your account 24/7 with out Client Online service

Benefits

  • Speed: access to cash within 24 hours to meet payments or make important purchases
  • Choice: full accounts receivables management support - we follow up with your customers for prompt payment
  • Flexibility: funding grows in line with your sales
  • Personal: expert advice from credit management professionals to help you evaluate potential customers
  • Convenience: real-time, online access to status reports via our account management system
  • Security: we offer protection when your customers have a financial inability to pay
  • Confidentiality: our confidential service means we do not let your customers know that you're using our service

Is your business suitable for Invoice Finance?

Your business could be suitable to use Invoice Finance if you:

  • Yes: provide goods and/or services to other businesses
  • Yes: are a business, trading with other businesses
  • Yes: send out invoices with payment terms of up to 90 days
  • Yes: are an established business or a start-up
  • Yes: are a company that is unable to obtain traditional bank financing or to increase an existing line of credit further

How Asset Finance can help your business

Asset Finance allows you to buy new equipment or machinery and spread the cost, helping to improve your cash flow. It is also a cost-effective way to raise money that you can use for any business purpose against the value of your current assets, while spreading the cost in manageable repayments. 

Asset Finance

Features

  • Unlock the value of your invoices and release additional funding
  • We can fund business assets from $1M to $10M
  • Spread your payments with monthly statements
  • Your company's assets will help generate the financing you need as we advance the funds against Accounts Receivable, Inventory, Machinery & Equipment, Real Estate

Benefits

  • Speed: buy equipment without the need for significant upfront costs
  • Cost effective: refinancing your assets can help you manage your cash flow more effectively
  • Efficient: leasing can help you spread the cost by paying in monthly installments
  • Experienced team: our Asset Finance team is here to support you and your business needs
  • Minimize risk: you can minimize the risks of ownership and benefit from tax efficiencies

Is your business suitable for Asset Finance?

Your business could be suitable to use Asset Finance if you:

  • Yes: own soft or hard assets
  • Yes: require the finance for business use
