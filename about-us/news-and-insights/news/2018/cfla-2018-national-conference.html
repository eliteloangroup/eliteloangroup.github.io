Connect with BFS at CFLA 2018 National Conference

The Canadian Finance & Leasing Association (CFLA) is hosting their Annual Conference on September 26-27 at Le Centre Sheraton in Montreal, Quebec. 

CFLA identifies itself as the only organization advocating the interests of the asset-based financing, vehicle and equipment leasing industry in Canada, giving members the ability to influence the shape of the industry’s future within the competitive financial services sector. CFLA holds a National Conference each fall that brings together approximately 400+ industry leaders to discuss business relevant information and to provide professional education to attendees. 

Bibby Financial Services Canada (BFS) will be attending this conference on September 26-27. This will be a great opportunity to discuss the alternative financial solutions that we can provide small- to medium-sized businesses, as well as our capabilities in asset-based lending, with anyone in attendance. 

You can click here for conference registration information. If you would like to schedule an appointment in advance to meet with us at the CFLA National Conference, please contact us. 

About Bibby Financial Services


Bibby Financial Services is a leading independent financial services partner to more than 10,250 businesses, providing more than $1.25 billion in funding annually and handling $11.6 billion in annual client turnover. With over 40 operations in 14 countries spanning 3 continents, we provide asset-based lending and factoring solutions to help businesses grow in domestic and international markets. Established in 2001, Bibby Financial Services supports businesses in virtually any industry. BFS is part of Bibby Line Group, a diverse and forward-looking family business with over 200 years’ experience of providing personal, responsive and flexible customer solutions. To find out more about Bibby Canada, please visit www.bibbycanada.ca.
