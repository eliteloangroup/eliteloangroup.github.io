Bibby Financial Services Canada Expands its Montreal Office

We are excited to share that our Montreal team is getting a new home! Bibby Financial Services (BFS) Canada is expanding to better serve small and mid-sized businesses in Montreal. 

Calum Williamson, Managing Director of BFS Canada said, “It’s important that we’re able to expand and adapt to the needs of our clients and business partners.  Our new central location, coupled with the expansion of our already experienced Business Development team in Montreal means we’re even better placed in supporting all of our stakeholders’ alternative finance needs in Eastern Canada.”

BFS Canada supports companies with alternative financing services including factoring, invoice discounting and asset-based lending.
